Scholarships


    INDIAN SCHOLARSHIPS:



  • National Talent Search Examination (NTSE) Scholarship:
    The NTSE scholarship is awarded by the National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT) to identify and support talented students in India. It is available for students studying in Class 10 and provides financial assistance throughout their educational journey.


  • Pre-Matric and Post-Matric Scholarships for Minority Students:
    These scholarships are offered by the Ministry of Minority Affairs, Government of India, to support students belonging to minority communities (such as Muslims, Christians, Sikhs, Buddhists, and Parsis) pursuing education at the pre-matric and post-matric levels.


  • Indian Oil Academic Scholarships:
    Indian Oil Corporation Limited provides scholarships to meritorious students pursuing professional courses in engineering, medical, and management streams. The scholarship aims to financially assist students and promote academic excellence.


  • JN Tata Endowment Scholarships:
    The JN Tata Endowment for Higher Education offers scholarships to Indian students who wish to pursue postgraduate studies or research abroad in various disciplines. It supports talented individuals to gain international exposure and contribute to their fields upon their return.


  • Kishore Vaigyanik Protsahan Yojana (KVPY):
    KVPY is a national-level scholarship program conducted by the Department of Science and Technology, Government of India. It encourages young students to pursue careers in research and basic sciences by providing financial support and mentorship.


  • Maulana Azad National Scholarship:
    This scholarship is provided by the Maulana Azad Education Foundation to economically disadvantaged students belonging to the minority communities. It aims to promote education among minority students by offering financial assistance for pursuing professional or technical courses.


  • Central Sector Scholarship Scheme for College and University Students:
    The Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India, awards scholarships to meritorious students from low-income families pursuing undergraduate and postgraduate courses in recognized colleges and universities.


  • Sitaram Jindal Foundation Scholarship:
    Sitaram Jindal Foundation offers scholarships to students studying in various disciplines, including engineering, medicine, nursing, and other professional courses. The scholarships are awarded based on merit and financial need.


  • Lady Meherbai D. Tata Education Trust Scholarships:
    These scholarships are provided by the Lady Meherbai D. Tata Education Trust to support Indian women pursuing higher education in India or abroad. The scholarships are available for various fields of study and help empower women through education.


  • Santoor Women's Scholarship:
    The Santoor Women's Scholarship program is sponsored by Wipro Consumer Care and Wipro Cares. It supports economically disadvantaged young women from economically weaker sections to pursue higher education in India.




    ABROAD SCHOLARSHIPS:



  • Commonwealth Scholarship and Fellowship Plan (CSFP):
    The CSFP offers scholarships to Indian students for pursuing master's and Ph.D. programs in Commonwealth countries. It covers tuition fees, living expenses, and airfare.


  • Inlaks Scholarship:
    The Inlaks Scholarship provides financial support to Indian students for pursuing postgraduate programs at top universities in the United States, United Kingdom, and Europe. The scholarship covers tuition fees, living expenses, and other allowances.


  • Charles Wallace India Trust Scholarships:
    The Charles Wallace India Trust offers scholarships to Indian students for short-term research, artistic residencies, or professional visits to the United Kingdom. It supports various disciplines, including arts, humanities, and social sciences.


  • Rhodes Scholarship:
    The Rhodes Scholarship is a highly prestigious international scholarship awarded to exceptional students from various countries, including India. It enables students to pursue postgraduate studies at the University of Oxford, covering tuition fees, living expenses, and travel costs.


  • Felix Scholarships:
    The Felix Scholarships are available for Indian students who wish to pursue postgraduate studies at the University of Oxford, University of Reading, or School of Oriental and African Studies (SOAS) in the United Kingdom. The scholarship covers tuition fees, living expenses, and airfare.


  • Dr. Manmohan Singh Scholarships:
    The Dr. Manmohan Singh Scholarships are awarded to Indian students pursuing postgraduate studies at the University of Cambridge, United Kingdom. The scholarship covers tuition fees, living expenses, and travel costs.


  • Erasmus Mundus Scholarships:
    Erasmus Mundus offers scholarships to Indian students for participating in joint master's and doctoral programs offered by a consortium of European universities. The scholarship covers tuition fees, living expenses, and travel costs.


  • Hornby Scholarships for English Language Teaching (ELT):
    The Hornby Scholarships, funded by the Hornby Educational Trust, provide Indian teachers of English an opportunity to pursue a master's degree in English Language Teaching in the United Kingdom. The scholarship covers tuition fees and living expenses.


  • Aga Khan Foundation International Scholarship Program:
    The Aga Khan Foundation provides scholarships to outstanding Indian students pursuing postgraduate studies in various disciplines at reputable universities in the United Kingdom, United States, and Canada. The scholarship covers tuition fees and living expenses.


  • University-specific Scholarships:
    Many universities worldwide offer scholarships specifically for international students, including Indian students. These scholarships vary in terms of eligibility criteria, coverage, and application processes. It is advisable to explore the scholarship options provided by the universities of interest.
